🏷️ Tagline:
"Saveik — Download TikTok videos without watermark, in HD. Free. No app needed."

📝 Description:
Saveik is the fastest, cleanest TikTok video downloader on the web. Paste a TikTok link and download any video in HD (1080p) without the TikTok watermark. Also supports MP3 audio extraction and TikTok Photo Mode downloads.

Why Saveik?
• No watermark — clean video ready for Instagram, YouTube, or any platform
• No app installation — works entirely in your browser
• No registration — completely free, unlimited downloads
• 20 languages — including Indonesian, Vietnamese, Thai, Spanish, Portuguese
• All devices — iPhone, Android, PC, Mac

Built with Next.js 15 and React 19. Deployed globally via Vercel + Cloudflare.
